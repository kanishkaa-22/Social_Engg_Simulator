🧠 What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information like passwords, OTPs, or bank details.

💥 What Happens If You Click a Phishing Link?

  • 🔓 You may be redirected to a fake login page
  • 🕵️ Attackers can steal your username & password
  • 💳 Your banking or personal data could be exposed
  • 📩 Your account may send spam without your knowledge
  • 🦠 Malware may get installed on your device

DATA THEFT • ACCOUNT HACK • FINANCIAL LOSS

🔄 What Just Happened (Simulation)

💡 Stay Safe

  • Check URLs carefully before clicking
  • Do not trust urgent emails blindly
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
  • Verify sender identity
